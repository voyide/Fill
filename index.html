How to play: Click a cell to draw its next diamond edge. Matching diagonal edges with the neighbor creates colored corner triangles and +2 points. Completing a diamond entirely in your color awards +4. Completing a 4-triangle star at a vertex awards +2.

Tip: Hover a cell to preview the next edge. Red is Human, Blue is AI. Game length: 2 minutes.
Special: Grey diamond shows a white circle. Clicking it pushes its 4 lines into the 4 diagonal neighbor cells (edge wraps). Swaps are visual; new fills credit the clicker only. Red/Blue diamond blasts clear edge-adjacent cells and themselves; matching-color destroyed lines give +1 each; center refills r,b,r,b and gets a new white circle.

Events: At 1:10 random blasts; at 1:00 gravity; at 0:10 random row swaps. Circles and blasts disabled during events 1 & 3.
